## 🎯 New Features

### 1. Intelligent Onboarding System
**Never lose a new user again!**

- **Smart Welcome Flow**: First-time users get contextual guidance at the perfect moment
- **Progressive Steps**: Learn features gradually without overwhelming
- **Visual Highlights**: Important elements pulse and glow to guide attention
- **Completion Tracking**: Remembers progress across sessions
- **Skip Anytime**: Users can dismiss or skip the tour

**How it works:**
- New users see a welcome tooltip when they first visit
- After loading their first model, they get camera control tips
- Features unlock progressively based on usage
- Celebrates milestones with encouraging messages

### 2. Advanced Mobile Gesture Controls
**Native app-like experience on mobile!**

- **Pinch to Zoom**: Natural two-finger zoom gesture
- **Two-Finger Rotate**: Rotate models with intuitive gestures
- **Swipe Navigation**: Swipe left/right to open/close sidebar
- **Long-Press Menu**: Hold to open context menu
- **Double-Tap Reset**: Quick camera reset
- **Haptic Feedback**: Subtle vibrations for tactile response

**Supported Gestures:**
- ✅ Tap - Select/interact
- ✅ Double-tap - Reset camera
- ✅ Long-press - Context menu
- ✅ Swipe - Navigate panels
- ✅ Pinch - Zoom in/out
- ✅ Rotate - Two-finger rotation
- ✅ Pan - Single-finger drag

### 3. Smart Feature Discovery
**AI-powered suggestions at the right time!**

- **Behavior Analysis**: Learns from how you use the app
- **Context-Aware**: Suggests features when you need them
- **Skill Tracking**: Adapts to beginner → intermediate → expert
- **Non-Intrusive**: Suggestions appear at natural moments
- **Dismissible**: Easy to ignore or defer suggestions

**Example Suggestions:**
- After loading 2 models → "Try lighting presets!"
- After 10 camera adjustments → "Use camera presets for quick angles"
- After 5 models → "Export screenshots to share your work"
- When skill increases → "Ready for Advanced mode?"

### 4. Beautiful, Accessible UI
**Looks great, works for everyone!**

- **Dark Mode**: Full dark theme support
- **Smooth Animations**: Buttery 60fps transitions
- **Touch-Friendly**: 44x44px minimum tap targets
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Full keyboard support
- **Screen Reader**: ARIA labels throughout
- **Reduced Motion**: Respects accessibility preferences
- **High Contrast**: Works in high contrast mode
